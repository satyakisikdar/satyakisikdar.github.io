About

I am a Graduate Student at the University of Notre Dame in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering under Dr. Tim Weninger.

My research centers on the intersection of formal language theory and network science. More specifically, I study graph generation models using hyperedge replacement grammars.

Prior to this, I worked on community detection in complex networks.
